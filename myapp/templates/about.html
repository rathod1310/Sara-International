Who We Are

We SARA INTERNATIONAL engaged in the business of processing, supplying & exporting of FOOD, SPICES, PULSES and GENERAL PROVISIONS, FMCG and GROCERY products which includes Rice, Wheat, Wheat Flour, Noodles, Kurkure, Namkeen, All types of Pooja material, Agarbatti, Many kind of Pooja kit and Many more range of Indian Spices.

We offer these products in various customized packaging that allows us to meet the specific requirements of our clients.

Our tie-ups with reputed suppliers & manufacturers help us to cater to the requirements of clients based in every corner of world.
